About Me

UX & UI Design // Data Visualization // Prototyping // by Christopher Chung



Hi, I'm Chris...


I was born and raised in New York, where I had the chance of experience both the exciting city of NYC and the beautiful nature of upstate NY. Before making the jump into UX design, I worked in IT. Due to my engineering background, I'm very passionate about understanding how things work and making the user experience as seamless and intuitive as possible. My strengths lie in interaction design, with a focus on prototyping, information architecture, and data visualization. You can usually find me outside exploring nature, hitting the slopes on a snowboard/skiis, or playing the saxophone.
