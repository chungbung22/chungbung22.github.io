MODAL/POPUP

3.18 INTRODUCTION

Modals/popups occur when a user selects a button or icon that triggers a form or confirmation/error message to popup.


There are four variations of the form: view, new, edit, and selector.


There are two variations for pop-ups: pop-up with message, and pop-up with question.


General rule of thumb is to avoid having a modal within a modal. Otherwise navigation becomes difficult.


3.18.1 View Form

The view form is used in cases where data needs to be viewed. The data presented will not be editable.
