CM and DME Unification

3.23 OVERVIEW

Although BWS-CM and BWS-DME are built on different platforms, there will be multiple users that use both applications in their day to day tasks. As a result, there is a need to unify the design between CM and DME, to ensure that users have a simple, and painless experience navigating between the two tools.


Since CM is web-based and built around google's material design UI, while DME is desktop-based and built around WPF, we have to choose either style or a mix of the two to follow. Given that material design is scalable and has pre-built widgets/kits, it'll be easier to adapt, compared to the custom built widgets for WPF.


Thus, the vision for CM and DME will be a unified, flat design, mirroring the look and feel of material design, as opposed to the skeuomorphic look and feel of WPF. However, the color palette used will be a mix of the two, since google's material design palette is too limiting to serve the needs of DME.
